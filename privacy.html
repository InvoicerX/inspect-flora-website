1. Information Collection

Inspect Flora does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, the application may collect limited technical information through third-party services, including device identifiers, crash logs, diagnostics, and app performance data. This information may be collected and processed by third-party service providers to support app functionality, improve stability, provide analytics, prevent fraud, and display advertisements.

2. Camera Permission

Inspect Flora requests access to your device camera (android.permission.CAMERA) solely to allow you to capture plant images for identification and to save entries in your Plant Journal.

Photos are taken only when you actively use the camera feature.

3. Plant Identification (Pl@ntNet API)

Inspect Flora uses the Pl@ntNet API to provide plant identification services.

When you choose to identify a plant, the photo you capture is securely transmitted to Pl@ntNet servers for analysis. This transmission is necessary to provide identification results.

Inspect Flora does not control how Pl@ntNet processes data. For more information, please review Pl@ntNet’s Privacy Policy:

https://plantnet.org/en/privacy-policy/

4. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Inspect Flora uses machine learning technologies for plant identification. Image processing may occur through the Pl@ntNet identification service.

5. Advertising and Analytics

Inspect Flora may use Google AdMob and Google Play Services.

These services may collect device identifiers, diagnostics information, crash data, app performance data, and other technical information necessary for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and service improvement.

Data collection and processing performed by these services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Internet Access

An internet connection is required to perform plant identification through the Pl@ntNet API and to load certain third-party services.

Inspect Flora does not sell users' personal information.

7. Data Storage

Plant Journal entries and saved plant information are stored locally on your device unless otherwise specified.

Inspect Flora does not maintain its own servers for storing Plant Journal entries or saved plant records.

8. Third-Party Services

Inspect Flora may use:

  • Pl@ntNet API
  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK)

These services operate under their own privacy policies and may collect data as described in their documentation.

9. Children’s Privacy

Inspect Flora is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Security

Data transmitted to third-party services is sent over secure HTTPS connections whenever supported by those services. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
