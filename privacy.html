Privacy Policy – Inspect Flora

Effective date: 2026-02-25

Inspect Flora is a free mobile application developed by AlenK.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the application.

1. Information Collection

Inspect Flora does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The application does not use analytics tools and does not collect advertising identifiers.

2. Camera Permission

Inspect Flora requests access to your device camera (android.permission.CAMERA) solely to allow you to capture plant images for identification and to save entries in your Plant Journal.

Photos are taken only when you actively use the camera feature.

3. Plant Identification (Pl@ntNet API)

Inspect Flora uses the Pl@ntNet API to provide plant identification services.

When you choose to identify a plant, the photo you capture is securely transmitted to Pl@ntNet servers for analysis. This transmission is necessary to provide identification results.

Inspect Flora does not control how Pl@ntNet processes data. For more information, please review Pl@ntNet’s Privacy Policy:

https://plantnet.org/en/privacy-policy/

4. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Inspect Flora uses machine learning technologies for plant identification. Image processing may occur through the Pl@ntNet identification service.

5. Internet Access

An internet connection is required to perform plant identification through the Pl@ntNet API.

Inspect Flora does not sell, trade, or use personal data for advertising purposes.

6. Data Storage

Plant Journal entries and saved plant information are stored locally on your device unless otherwise specified.

Inspect Flora does not maintain its own servers for storing user data.

7. Third-Party Services

Inspect Flora may use:

  • Pl@ntNet API
  • Google Play Services

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Inspect Flora does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Security

Data transmitted to Pl@ntNet is sent over secure HTTPS connections. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The most recent version will always be available on this page.
